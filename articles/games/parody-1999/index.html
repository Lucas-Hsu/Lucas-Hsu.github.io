Parody 1999

Embark on a dangerous mission with your friends and level up to become overpowered and defeat the Bad Evil Group!

This is a simple turn-based card game I created to make fun of a game I thoroughly enjoyed. That is to say, I liked Reverse: 1999 despite its gacha mechanics, so I was inspired to make a game that kept the parts I enjoyed while emphasizing the unsatisfactory parts of the game so that it may be ridiculous to the point of being hilarious. Back in 2023–2024, this game was quite beloved by me and my friends, so I put in some inside jokes which I’m afraid will be confusing to outside eyes. You can still enjoy the Blender-rendered cutscenes and cool particle effects though!

In February 2026, I uploaded a web version so you can play the game in your browser. Sadly, this version does not support playing Unity VideoClips, so you’ll be missing out on a substantial part of the game’s intended experience. Try out the downloadable version!
