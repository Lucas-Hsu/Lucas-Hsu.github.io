Merge Game

This is my take on the watermellon/merging game, with a focus on a “turn your brain off” style relaxing and satisfying gameplay.

Back in November 2023, I found out about Suika Game and its derivatives from my friends. It is a simple game, so re-making it was the perfect opportunity for me to learn Unity through a hands-on, practical experience. In January 2026, I updated the game to add a simple toggle-able background music track and a few more tips onscreen.
